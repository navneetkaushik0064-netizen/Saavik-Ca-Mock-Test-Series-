Cumulative vs 100% Syllabus

Understand which test series is right for your stage of preparation — or combine both for maximum impact.

100% Syllabus Test Series
A single comprehensive test covering the entire syllabus. Ideal for a final assessment close to the examination date when preparation is already complete.
Cumulative Test Series — 3 Phases
Three progressive tests with increasing syllabus coverage — ideal for systematic revision, building exam confidence gradually over time, and reinforcing critical topics through repetition.

Recommended: Combine both for the most comprehensive CA exam preparation
